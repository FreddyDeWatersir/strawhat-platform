## Capital & Italian VAT (brief)

- **First case budget:** ~€3,000–€5,000 (product + shipping + IVA buffer + OP-18 deposit room)
- **Decide before scaling:** occasional private sale vs *regime forfettario* (5% flat under €85k) vs full VAT
- **Imports >€150:** ~22% IVA + ~3–4% duty on declared value
